Lemon Tea Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup of water
  2. 1 tea bag (black or green tea)
  3. 1 tablespoon of honey (optional)
  4. Juice of half a lemon
  5. Slices of lemon for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Boil the water in a pot or kettle.
  2. Once the water is boiled, pour it into a cup and add the tea bag. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes, or according to your taste preference.
  3. Remove the tea bag and add honey if desired. Stir well to dissolve the honey.
  4. Squeeze the juice of half a lemon into the tea and stir again.
  5. Garnish with lemon slices for an extra touch.
  6. Your refreshing lemon tea is ready to enjoy!
