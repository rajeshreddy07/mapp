Pulav Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup Basmati rice
  2. 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  3. 1 small onion, sliced
  4. 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  5. Whole spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves)
  6. Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans) - optional
  7. 2 cups water
  8. Salt to taste
  9. Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Rinse and soak the Basmati rice for 20-30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and sauté sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add whole spices and sauté for another minute.
  5. Optional: Add mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
  6. Add drained rice and sauté for a couple of minutes.
  7. Pour in water, add salt, and give it a stir.
  8. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  9. Fluff the rice with a fork.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  11. Enjoy your delicious pulao!
