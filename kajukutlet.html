Kajucutlet Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup cashews (kaju), soaked for 2-3 hours
  2. 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  3. 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  4. 1/4 cup milk
  5. 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  6. A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
  7. Silver vark (edible silver foil) for garnish (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Grind the soaked cashews to a fine paste using very little water. This will be the cashew base for the sweet.
  2. In a pan, heat ghee and add the cashew paste. Cook on low heat, stirring continuously.
  3. Once the cashew paste starts to thicken, add powdered sugar and continue to stir.
  4. Add milk gradually while stirring to achieve a smooth consistency. Continue cooking until the mixture leaves the sides of the pan.
  5. Add cardamom powder and saffron strands (if using). Mix well.
  6. Remove the mixture from heat and allow it to cool slightly.
  7. Grease your hands with ghee and shape the mixture into small, flat cutlets.
  8. Garnish the kaju cutlet sweets with silver vark if desired.
  9. Allow the cutlets to cool completely before serving.
