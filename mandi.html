Mandi Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups basmati rice, washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  2. 500g lamb or chicken, cut into pieces
  3. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  4. 4 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 2 tomatoes, chopped
  6. 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  7. 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  8. 1/4 cup cooking oil
  9. 2 tablespoons Mandi spice mix (available in stores or homemade)
  10. 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  11. 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  12. 1/2 teaspoon ground black lime (loomi)
  13. 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  14. Salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a large oven-safe pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Stir in Mandi spice mix, ground cumin, ground coriander, and ground black lime. Cook for a couple of minutes.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  6. Place the pieces of lamb or chicken into the pot and brown them on all sides.
  7. Add chopped cilantro and mint leaves. Season with salt to taste.
  8. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  9. Stir in the soaked basmati rice. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid.
  10. Transfer the pot to the preheated oven and bake for 30-40 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the meat is tender.
  11. Once done, fluff the rice with a fork, and serve the Mandi hot.
