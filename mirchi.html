Mirchi Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 8-10 large green chilies (bhavnagri or any less spicy variety)
  2. 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  3. 1/4 cup rice flour
  4. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  5. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  6. A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  7. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  8. Salt to taste
  9. Water (for batter)
  10. Oil for deep frying

Procedure:

  1. Wash the green chilies and slit them lengthwise. Remove seeds if you want it less spicy.
  2. In a bowl, mix gram flour, rice flour, red chili powder, turmeric powder, asafoetida, cumin seeds, and salt. . Add water gradually to make a smooth, thick batter. Ensure there are no lumps.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan or kadhai for frying.
  4. Dip each chili in the batter, ensuring it is well coated.
  5. Carefully slide the coated chilies into the hot oil. Fry on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crisp.
  6. Remove the fried chilies using a slotted spoon and place them on paper towels to absorb excess oil.
  7. Mirchi Bhaji is ready to be served. Enjoy it with green chutney or sauce.
