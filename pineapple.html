Pineapple Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 ripe pineapple
  2. Water (optional, for dilution)
  3. Ice cubes (optional, for serving)

Procedure:

  1. Start by preparing the pineapple. Cut off the top and bottom of the pineapple, then carefully remove the outer skin.
  2. Once the skin is removed, cut the pineapple into smaller pieces for easier blending.
  3. Place the pineapple pieces in a blender or food processor.
  4. Blend the pineapple until it becomes a smooth puree.
  5. If you prefer a thinner consistency, you can add a little water to the blender and blend again. This step is optional.
  6. Once blended, strain the pineapple puree through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove any fibrous bits. This step is also optional, depending on your preference.
