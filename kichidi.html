Khichdi Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup split yellow moong dal
  2. 1 cup rice
  3. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  4. 1 large tomato, chopped
  5. 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans), chopped
  6. 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  7. 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  8. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  9. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  10. 2 tablespoons ghee or cooking oil
  11. A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  12. 4 cups water
  13. Salt to taste
  14. Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Wash the rice and dal together under running water until the water runs clear. Soak them in water for about 30 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pressure cooker. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Allow them to splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Stir in asafoetida, turmeric powder, and red chili powder (if using). Mix well.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate.
  6. Add mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Drain the soaked rice and dal, and add them to the pressure cooker. Mix everything together.
  8. Pour in 4 cups of water and add salt to taste. Stir well.
  9. Close the pressure cooker with its lid and cook on medium heat. After the first whistle, reduce the heat to low and cook for about 10-12 minutes.
  10. Let the pressure release naturally before opening the cooker.
  11. Garnish the khichdi with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
