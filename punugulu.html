Punugulu Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup idli batter
  2. 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  3. 2 tablespoons finely chopped green chilies
  4. 2 tablespoons finely chopped curry leaves
  5. 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  6. 1/4 teaspoon baking soda (optional)
  7. Salt to taste
  8. Oil for deep frying

Procedure:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine idli batter, chopped onions, chopped green chilies, chopped curry leaves, cumin seeds, baking soda (if using), and salt.
  2. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Once the oil is hot, drop small portions of the batter into the oil using a spoon or your hands to form round shapes.
  4. Fry the punugulu until they turn golden brown and crispy, turning them occasionally for even cooking.
  5. Remove the punugulu from the oil and place them on paper towels to absorb excess oil.
  6. Serve the punugulu hot with coconut chutney or any dipping sauce of yourÂ choice.
