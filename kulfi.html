Kulfi Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups full-fat milk
  2. 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  3. 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  4. 1/4 cup milk powder
  5. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  6. 2 tablespoons chopped nuts (pistachios, almonds, cashews)
  7. 1 tablespoon cornstarch or arrowroot powder (optional, for thickening)

Procedure:

  1. In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat the full-fat milk over medium heat, stirring continuously to prevent it from sticking to the bottom.
  2. Once the milk comes to a boil, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer, stirring occasionally, until it reduces to about half its original quantity.
  3. Add sweetened condensed milk, powdered sugar, and milk powder. Stir well to combine.
  4. If you want a thicker consistency, dissolve cornstarch or arrowroot powder in a little water and add it to the milk mixture. Stir continuously until it thickens.
  5. Add cardamom powder and chopped nuts. Mix well and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Remove the mixture from heat and let it cool to room temperature.
  7. Pour the cooled mixture into kulfi molds or small cups. Cover with aluminum foil or lids.
  8. Place the molds or cups in the freezer for at least 6 hours or overnight until the kulfi sets.
  9. Once set, run the molds or cups under warm water for a few seconds to loosen the kulfi. Gently unmold and serve.
