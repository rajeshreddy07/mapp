Rasmalai Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 litre milk
  2. 1 lemon
  3. pinch of baking powder
  4. 1 litre water
  5. 1 glass sugar

Procedure:

  1. Boil milk and curdle it with lemon juice or vinegar.
  2. Strain and rinse the curds, then knead them.
  3. Shape the curds into balls.
  4. Make a sugar syrup and cook the balls in it.
  5. Simmer milk with sugar and cardamom.
  6. Soak the cooked balls in the milk.
  7. Chill and garnish with nuts.
