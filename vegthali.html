Thali Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. One or two vegetable dishes to be eaten with chapati or roti, and
  2. One dal or kadhi is eaten with rice.
  3. This is served along with one bowl of cumin-flavored yogurt or raita.
  4. 1Pickle and papadum are appetizers that usually don’t need cooking but become essential with every Indian meal.

Procedure:

  1. To prepare a veg thali, you can follow these general guidelines: Ingredients: - Rice or roti - Vegetable curry or sabzi - Dal - Raita or yogurt - Salad - Pickles or chutney - Papad Instructions:
  2. Cook the rice or prepare the roti as per your preference and keep it aside.
  3. Prepare a vegetable curry or sabzi by sautéing your choice of vegetables with spices such as cumin, turmeric, coriander, and garam masala.
  4. Cook the dal of your choice (e.g., lentils) with water and spices until soft and flavorful.
  5. Prepare raita by mixing yogurt with grated cucumber, salt, cumin powder, and chopped coriander leaves.
  6. Assemble a simple salad with chopped cucumber, tomatoes, and onions. You can also add a squeeze of lemon and sprinkle some salt.
  7. Serve with pickles or chutney of your choice and papad.
  8. Arrange all the prepared items in small bowls or plates and serve together to form a complete thali. You can customize the thali based on your preferences and the availability of ingredients. Enjoy your veg.
