Veg Biryani Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  2. 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans), chopped
  3. 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  4. 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  5. 1/4 cup cashews, fried
  6. 1/4 cup raisins
  7. 2 tomatoes, chopped
  8. 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  9. 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  10. 4 cups water
  11. 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  12. 2 tablespoons oil
  13. 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  14. 1 teaspoon biryani masala
  15. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  16. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  17. Whole spices (1 bay leaf, 4-5 cloves, 4-5 green cardamom, 1 cinnamon stick)
  18. Salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil and ghee in a pan. Add whole spices and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste, chopped tomatoes, and cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add mixed vegetables, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Mix in yogurt, chopped mint, and cilantro. Cook until the vegetables are partially cooked.
  6. Meanwhile, boil water in a separate pot. Add soaked basmati rice and cook until 70% done. Drain the rice.
  7. Layer the partially cooked vegetable mixture and partially cooked rice in a heavy-bottomed pan. Sprinkle fried cashews and raisins.
  8. Cover the pan with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and aromatic.
  9. Fluff the biryani with a fork. Serve hot, garnished with additional fried onions, if desired.
