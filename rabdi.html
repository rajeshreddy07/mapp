Rabdi Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 liter full-fat milk
  2. 1/2 cup sugar
  3. A pinch of saffron strands (soaked in warm milk)
  4. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  5. Chopped nuts for garnish (almonds, pistachios)

Procedure:

  1. Pour the milk into a heavy-bottomed pan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Once it boils, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer. Stir occasionally to prevent the milk from sticking to the bottom.
  3. As the milk simmers, a cream layer will form on top. Gently push it to the sides of the pan.
  4. Continue simmering and pushing the cream layer to the sides. Scrape the sides and add it back to the milk. Repeat this process until the milk reduces to about half.
  5. Add sugar and stir well. Continue simmering until the Rabdi thickens to the desired consistency.
  6. Mix in saffron-soaked milk and cardamom powder. Stir and cook for a few more minutes.
  7. Let the Rabdi cool to room temperature.
  8. Garnish with chopped nuts.
  9. Serve chilled or at room temperature. Enjoy your homemade Rabdi!
