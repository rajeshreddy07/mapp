Coffee Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup of water
  2. 2 tablespoons of coffee grounds
  3. Sugar or sweetener (optional)
  4. Milk or cream (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Boil the water in a pot or kettle.
  2. While the water is boiling, add the coffee grounds to a coffee filter or a French press.
  3. Once the water is boiled, pour it over the coffee grounds.
  4. Let the coffee brew for about 4-5 minutes for a medium strength.Adjust the time to your preference.
  5. If desired, add sugar or sweetener to taste.
  6. Stir well to dissolve the sugar.
  7. If you like your coffee with milk or cream, add it now and stir again.
  8. Give it a taste and adjust the sweetness or creaminess according to your liking.
  9. Your delicious cup of coffee is ready to enjoy!
