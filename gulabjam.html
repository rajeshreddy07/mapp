Gulab Jamun Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup of milk powder
  2. 2 tbsps ghee
  3. pinch of baking powder
  4. 1/4 cup of all purpose flour
  5. 1 cup water
  6. 1 cup sugar
  7. 1/4 tsp saffron strands
  8. 3 tsp of milk
  9. Ghee or oil for deep frying

Procedure:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine 1 cup of milk powder, 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour, and a pinch of baking soda.
  2. Gradually add 2 tablespoons of ghee (clarified butter) and mix until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  3. Slowly pour in 2-3 tablespoons of milk and knead the mixture into a soft dough. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, in a deep saucepan, heat oil or ghee for frying on medium heat.
  5. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth balls without any cracks.
  6. Carefully drop the balls into the hot oil and fry them until they turn golden brown. Make sure to fry them in batches for even cooking.
  7. Once fried, remove the Gulab Jamuns from the oil and let them cool slightly.
  8. In a separate saucepan, combine 1 cup of sugar, 1 cup of water, and a few strands of saffron. Bring it to a boil and let the sugar dissolve completely.
  9. Add a few drops of rose water or cardamom powder to the sugar syrup for flavor.
  10. Gently place the fried Gulab Jamuns into the warm sugar syrup and let them soak for at least 30 minutes.
  11. Serve the Gulab Jamuns warm or at room temperature. They taste delicious on their own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream!
