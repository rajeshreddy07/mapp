DRINKS

GINGER TEA

                    A delicious, warming Indian Ginger Tea made with fresh ginger roots, tea leaves and milk. 
                    This tea has a wonderful aroma and will have you coming back for more! 
                    It also includes many health benefits.

TOTAL TIME: 10 MINS

INGREDIENTS:

  • Milk or Water
  • lemon
  • Tea powder
  • Honey or Sugar
  • Sliced Ginger

PREPARATION:

  1. Put the ginger root piece directly in a mug.
  2. Add the boiling water (or milk) and steep for 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Add honey or lemon juice to taste, if desired.
