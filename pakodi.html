Pakodi Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  2. 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  3. 1/4 cup finely chopped spinach (optional)
  4. 1/4 cup finely chopped potatoes
  5. 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  6. 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  7. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  8. 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  9. 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  10. 1/4 teaspoon baking soda (optional, for extra crispiness)
  11. Salt to taste
  12. Water, as needed, to make the batter
  13. Oil for deep frying

Procedure:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine chickpea flour, chopped onions, chopped spinach (if using), chopped potatoes, ginger-garlic paste, cumin seeds, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, baking soda (if using), and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to form a thick batter. Ensure there are no lumps in the batter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Once the oil is hot, drop small portions of the batter into the oil using a spoon or your hands.
  5. Fry the pakodis until they turn golden brown and crispy, turning them occasionally for even cooking.
  6. Remove the pakodis from the oil and place them on paper towels to absorb excess oil.
  7. Serve the pakodis hot with mint chutney or tomatoÂ ketchup.
