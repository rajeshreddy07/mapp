Jelebi Preparation

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  2. 2 tbsp chickpea flour (besan)
  3. 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. 1/4 tsp turmeric powder (for color)
  5. 1 cup warm water
  6. 1 1/2 cups sugar
  7. 1 cup water
  8. 1/4 tsp saffron strands
  9. 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  10. Ghee or oil for deep frying

Procedure:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, chickpea flour, baking powder, and turmeric powder.
  2. Gradually add the warm water and mix to form a smooth batter. The consistency should be similar to pancake batter. Cover the bowl and let the batter rest for about 12 hours or overnight in a warm place. This helps in fermenting the batter slightly.
  3. After the fermentation period, the batter will become slightly frothy and bubbly.
  4. In a separate saucepan, prepare the sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water over medium heat. Add saffron strands and cardamom.
